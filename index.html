Donald Cooks Soup

The Legendary Soup Story

One day, Donald Trump decided he wanted to become a chef. He confidently walked into the kitchen and announced, "Today, I will make the greatest soup the world has ever seen!"

Donald began throwing ingredients into the pot: a chicken leg, a few carrots, ten potatoes, and even a sprinkle of gold dust, "for luxury," he said.

An hour later, his assistant tasted the soup and turned pale. "Sir," he said, "you didn't make soup... you made a weapon!"

Donald smiled and replied, "Exactly! It's so strong, it'll stop anything. We're going to make soup great again!"
